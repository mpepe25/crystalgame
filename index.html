Crystal Collector!

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below.By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total scroe.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you loose the game if your total score goes above random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
